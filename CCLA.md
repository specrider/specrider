# SpecRider Corporate Contributor License Agreement

Thank you for your interest in contributing to SpecRider, a project of
**805 Software LLC** ("805 Software", "we", or "us").

This Corporate Contributor License Agreement ("Agreement") documents the rights
granted by a contributing organization to 805 Software. It is intended for use by an
entity (the **"Corporation"**) whose employees or agents contribute to SpecRider
in the course of their work.

> **This Agreement is supplemental — it does not replace individual signing.**
> Each of the Corporation's Designated Employees must still sign the
> [Individual CLA](CLA.md) through the automated CLA workflow on their first pull
> request (that is the signature the merge check enforces). This Corporate CLA
> sits on top of those individual signatures to confirm that the Corporation —
> which may own the IP its employees create — also grants the same rights. This
> mirrors the Apache Software Foundation's model, where a Corporate CLA does not
> remove the need for each contributor to have an Individual CLA on file.

## How to submit

To keep corporate and personal-contact details out of public git history, **do not
submit a completed Corporate CLA via pull request.** Instead, fill out the
signature block and Schedule A below and email the signed document (PDF or scan)
to:

> cla@specrider.ai

The maintainer will confirm receipt and record which GitHub usernames from
Schedule A are covered. Remember that each listed employee must also sign the
[Individual CLA](CLA.md) through the CLA workflow on their first pull request — the
Corporate CLA does not bypass that check.
